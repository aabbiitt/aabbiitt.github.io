Welcome to Maya International School

Maya International School Ethiopia is dedicated to delivering an exceptional British curriculum education, catering to students from Early Years through to A Levels and BTEC programs. As a Pearson Edexcel accredited institution, we are deeply committed to fostering a student-centered learning environment that not only emphasizes academic excellence but also encourages the development of critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. Our holistic approach ensures that each student is equipped with the knowledge and abilities needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving global landscape. We take pride in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to excel in both their academic pursuits and future careers.

ABOUT US

Producing Global Leaders

Welcome to Maya International School Ethiopia (MISE), a vibrant community that supports over 1,000 students, guided by passionate staff and actively involved parents. Our diverse family, deeply rooted in Ethiopia, reaches out globally through the National Curriculum for England. At MISE, we are dedicated to shaping future leaders, preparing our graduates to excel both locally and on the international stage. We warmly invite prospective students, parents, and staff to discover the dynamic world of MISE, where endless opportunities are within reach. Become a part of our respected community and embark on an educational journey that will transform your future.

Celebrating Student Achievements

Our school prides itself on fostering a culture of academic excellence and personal growth. Through our diverse curriculum and dedicated teaching staff, students are encouraged to explore their interests and achieve their full potential. Our programs are designed to prepare students for both local and international opportunities, equipping them with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their future endeavors. At Maya International School, we celebrate the unique achievements of each student, whether in academics, sports, or the arts, and we are committed to supporting their journey toward becoming well-rounded global citizens.
